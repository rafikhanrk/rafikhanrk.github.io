Introduction

Washington State has consistently ranked among the top states for refugee resettlement in the United States. This section provides an overview of refugee immigration statistics, demographics, and trends over time.

Confrontation

Rainier Valley

Rainier Beach has a diverse cultural landscape with a notable presence of refugees from Somalia, Vietnam, Ethiopia, and other countries. Community organizations provide vital support through housing, legal assistance, and cultural integration programs.

Columbia City

Columbia City, part of Rainier Valley, is notable for its diverse population, including many immigrants and refugees. The neighborhood offers a range of community organizations and cultural centers that support the refugee population, providing resources such as housing, legal assistance, and cultural integration programs.
