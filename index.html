Refugee Housing Crisis in Washington State

Introduction

Washington State has consistently ranked among the top states for refugee resettlement in the United States. This section provides an overview of refugee immigration statistics, demographics, and trends over time.

Confrontation

Rainier Valley

Rainier Valley is one of Seattle's most ethnically diverse neighborhoods, with a significant refugee population in Rainier Beach.

Tukwila

Tukwila, located south of Seattle, has a high concentration of refugees, particularly around Riverton Park United Methodist Church.

Resolution

This section summarizes the key findings and discusses the impact of the refugee influx on crime rates, housing, and cultural integration in Rainier Beach and Tukwila.
